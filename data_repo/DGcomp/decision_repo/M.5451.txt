1.   On 11 February 2009 the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration
     pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 by which the undertaking
     CNP Assurances S.A. ("CNP Assurances", France) acquires within the meaning of Article
     3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation sole control of CNP Unicredit Life, Ltd. ("CNP Unicredit
     Life", Ireland) by way of purchase of shares.
2.   The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
        -        for CNP Assurances: life and non-life insurance products in a number of countries
such as France, Italy, Portugal, Spain amongst others;
        -        for CNP Unicredit Life: life insurance in Italy.
3.   After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
     operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 and of paragraph
