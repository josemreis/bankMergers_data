Dear Sirs,
Subject:      Case No. COMP/M.2971 – AEGON / LA MONDIALE
              Notification of 12/11/2002 pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC)
              No. 4064/891
              Publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities No. C 288 of
              23/11/2002, page 8
1.   On 12/11/2002, the Commission received notification of a proposed concentration pursuant
     to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89 by which the Dutch undertaking
     AEGON International N.V. belonging to the Dutch based AEGON group (“AEGON”) and the
     French based La Mondiale group (“La Mondiale”) acquire within the meaning of Article
     3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of the French undertaking La Mondiale
     Participations by way of purchase of shares and assets.
2.   The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
        - AEGON:                    international insurance group
        - La Mondiale:              life insurance and pension product

3.  After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
    operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89 and of paragraph
    4, subparagraph c of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of
    certain concentrations under Council Regulation (EEC) No 4064/892.
4.  For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the Commission has decided
    not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the common market
    and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of Article 6(1)(b) of
    Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89.
