Subject:      Case No. COMP/M.2984 - SOFINCO/Commercial Bank of Greece/JV
              Notification of 26.09.2002 pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC)
              No. 4064/891
              Publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities No. C N° 238
              of 03.10.2002, p. 15.
1. On 26/09/2002, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to
   Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC) No 4064/89 by which the French undertaking SOFINCO
   S.A. belonging to the Credit Agricole Group and the Greek undertaking Commercial Bank of
   Greece S.A. belonging to the Commercial Bank Group acquire within the meaning of Article
   3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of the Greek undertaking Emporiki Credicom
   Bank S.A. by way of purchase of shares in a newly created company constituting a joint
   venture.
2. The business activities of the undertakings concerned are :
        -        for SOFINCO S.A.: consumer financing in France (and other countries)
        -        for Credit Agricole Group: banking services and products
        -        for Commercial Bank of Greece S.A.: banking services and products in Greece (and
                 other countries)

       -       for Commercial Bank Group: banking services and products
       -       for Emporiki Credicom Bank S.A.: consumer financing in Greece.
3. After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
   operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89 and of paragraph
   4, subparagraph (b) of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of
   certain concentrations under Council Regulation (EEC) No 4064/892.
4. For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the Commission has decided
   not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the common market
   and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of Article 6(1)(b) of
   Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89.
