                No 139/2004 and Article 57 of the Agreement on the European Economic Area
1.      On 28.04.2015, the European Commission received notification of a proposed
        concentration pursuant to Article 4 of the Merger Regulation by which the undertaking
        Advent International Corporation (‘Advent’, United States of America) will acquire within
        the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Merger Regulation sole control of the undertaking
        Hypo Group Alpe Adria AG (‘HGAA’, Austria), by way purchase of shares.3
2.      The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
        <U+F02D>    Advent: a global private equity investor in various sectors, including media,
             communications, financial services, information technology, retail, industrial and
             pharmaceuticals;

        retail, corporate and public customers and (ii) leasing business in South-Eastern Europe,

3. After examination of the notification, the European Commission has concluded that the
   notified operation falls within the scope of the Merger Regulation and of paragraph 5(c) of
   the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of certain concentrations
   under Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004.4
4. For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the European Commission
   has decided not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the
   internal market and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of
   Article 6(1)(b) of the Merger Regulation and Article 57 of the EEA Agreement.
