Dear Sir or Madam,
1.      On 13/11/2018, the European Commission received notification of a proposed
        concentration pursuant to Article 4 of the Merger Regulation by which Fairfax Financial
        Holdings Limited (‘Fairfax’, Canada) acquires, within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the
        Merger Regulation, sole control of the whole of Eurolife Erb Insurance Group Holdings
        S.A. (‘Eurolife’, Greece) by way of purchase of shares.3
2.      The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
        <U+F02D>     for Fairfax: a holding company active in property and casualty insurance and
              reinsurance and investment management.
        <U+F02D>     for Eurolife: a holding company active in life and non-life insurance and reinsurance
              and insurance distribution in Greece and Romania.
3.      After examination of the notification, the European Commission has concluded that the
        notified operation falls within the scope of the Merger Regulation and of paragraph 5(c)
        and (d) of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of certain
        concentrations under Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004.4
4.      For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the European Commission
        has decided not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the
