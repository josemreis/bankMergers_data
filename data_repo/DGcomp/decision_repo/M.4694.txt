1.   On 24.05.2007, the Commission received notification of a proposed concentration pursuant
     to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/2004 by which the undertaking RCI
     Banque ("RCI" France) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council
     Regulation control of the whole of the undertaking RFS Limited ("RFS" United Kingdom)
     by way of purchase of shares. RCI currently holds joint control over RFS.

Commission européenne, B-1049 Bruxelles / Europese Commissie, B-1049 Brussel - Belgium. Telephone: (32-2) 299 11 11.

2. The business activities of the undertakings concerned are :
      -       for RCI: automobile financing and services for end customers and dealers of
              Renault, Nissan and Dacia vehicles in several Member States, including in the
              UK;
      -       for RFS: automobile financing and services for the end customers and dealer of
              Renault vehicles in the UK only.
3. After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
   operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/2004 and of paragraph
   5, subparagraph d of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of
   certain concentrations under Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/20042.
4. For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the Commission has decided
   not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the common market
   and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of Article 6(1)(b) of
   Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/2004.
