1.      On 16 June 2016, the European Commission received notification of a proposed
        concentration pursuant to Article 4 of the Merger Regulation by which funds managed by
        Goldman Sachs Asset Management, L.P (“GSAM Funds”, USA) and funds managed by
        Deutsche Alternative Asset Management (Global) Limited (“DAAM”, Germany), acquire
        within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Merger Regulation joint control over the
        interests held by the National Bank of Greece S.A. in eleven UK and Guersney funds (the
        "Target").3
2.      The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
             <U+F02D> GSAM: provision of investment advisory services and asset management, globally.
             <U+F02D> DAAM: investment management and fund management services, globally.
             <U+F02D> the Target: private equity funds with interests in various portfolio companies,
                 primarily active in healthcare, food/beverages, industrials, services and real estate,
                 in Western, South Eastern and Central Europe as well as Turkey. The equity funds
