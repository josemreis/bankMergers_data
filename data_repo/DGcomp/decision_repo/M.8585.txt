Dear Sir or Madam,
1.      On 13 September 2017, the European Commission received notification of a proposed
        concentration pursuant to Article 4 of the Merger Regulation by which Axis Capital
        Holdings Limited (Bermuda) (‘Axis’) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the
        Merger Regulation control of the whole of Novae Group PLC (United Kingdom) (‘Novae’)
        by way of public bid.3
2.      The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
             <U+F02D> for Axis: a Bermuda headquartered global (re)insurance company listed on the New
                 York Stock Exchange providing a broad range of specialty lines insurance and
                 treaty reinsurance,
             <U+F02D>    for Novae: a United Kingdom publically listed company headquartered in London
                 and listed on the London Stock Exchange; through its syndicate at Lloyd’s
                 (Syndicate 2007), it underwrites both insurance and reinsurance.
3.      After examination of the notification, the European Commission has concluded that the
        notified operation falls within the scope of the Merger Regulation and of paragraph 5(c) of
        the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of certain concentrations
        under Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004.4
