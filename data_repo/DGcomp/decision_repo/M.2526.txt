Subject: Case No. COMP/M.2526 - GE Insurance Holdings/National Mutual Life
              Notification of 24 August 2001 pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC)
              No. 4064/891
              Publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities No. C 246,
              4.9.2001, p.2
1.     On 24.08.2001, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to
       Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC) No 4064/892 by which the U.K. based undertaking GE
       Insurance Holdings Limited (“GE Insurance”) belonging to the U.S. group General Electric
       Company (“GE”) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation
       control of the whole of the U.K. based National Mutual Life Assurance Society (“National
       Mutual”) by way of purchase of assets.

2.  The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
       - for GE Insurance: insurance and savings business
       - for GE:                diversified industrial corporation active in numerous fields including
                                aircraft engines, transportation systems, industrial systems, power
                                systems, plastics, lighting, medical systems, appliances, media,
                                financial services, software and Internet services.
       - for National Mutual: life insurance and pension products
3.  After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
    operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89 and of paragraph
    4, subparagraph c of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of
    certain concentrations under Council Regulation (EEC) No 4064/893.
4.  For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the Commission has decided
    not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the common market
    and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of Article 6(1)(b) of
    Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89.
