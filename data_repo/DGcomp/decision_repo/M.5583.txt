1.  On 18/08/2009, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration
    pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 by which the undertaking
    PartnerRe Ltd (“PartnerRe”, Bermuda) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the
    Council Regulation sole control over Paris Re Holdings Limited (“Paris Re”, Switzerland),
    by way of purchase of shares.
2.  The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
      - PartnerRe : global reinsurance company, listed on New York Stock Exchange,
          providing multi-line reinsurance to insurance companies;
      - Paris Re : French-listed, worldwide reinsurer.
3.  After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
    operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 and of paragraph
