1.  On 13 June 2007, the Commission received notification of a proposed concentration
    pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/2004 by which Mapfre S.A
    ("Mapfre", Spain) and Societá Cattolica di Assicurazioni soc.coop. ("Cattolica", Italy) acquire
    within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of Mapfre
    Cattolica Auto S.p.A. ("JV", Italy) by way of contribution of assets and purchase of shares.
2.  The business activities of the undertakings concerned are :
        -        for Mapfre: insurance services, primarily in Spain;
        -        for for Cattolica: insurance services, primarily in Italy;
        -        for JV: motor vehicle liability insurance services in Italy.

3. After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
   operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/2004 and of paragraph
   5, c (i) of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of certain
   concentrations under Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/20042.
4. For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the Commission has decided
   not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the common market
   and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of Article 6(1)(b) of
   Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/2004.
