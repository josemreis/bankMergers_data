                                                                 To the notifying party
Dear Sir,
Subject: Case No. COMP/M.3997- SUN CAPITAL / SARA LEE
            Notification of 26.10.2005 pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/20041
            Publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities No. C 277, 10.11.2005,
            page 11.
1.       On 26.10.2005, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant
         to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 by which the undertaking […] Sun
         Group (“Sun”, USA), acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council
         Regulation control of the European Branded Apparel division (“The Business”) of Sara Lee
         Corporation (USA), by way of purchase of shares.
2.       The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
         -        for Sun : group of private equity investment funds;
         -        for The Business: design, manufacture, distribution and sale in Europe of branded
                  apparel products, such as women’s intimates, men’s underwear, hosiery and socks.

  3.     After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
         operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EC) No.139/2004 and of
         paragraph 5, subparagraph b, of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for
         treatment of certain concentrations under Council Regulation (EC) No 139/20042.
  4.     For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the Commission has
         decided not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the
         common market and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of
         Article 6(1)(b) of Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/2004.
