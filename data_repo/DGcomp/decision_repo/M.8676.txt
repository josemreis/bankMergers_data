Dear Sir or Madam,
1.      On 12 October 2017, the European Commission received notification of a proposed
        concentration pursuant to Article 4 of the Merger Regulation by which Hellman &
        Friedman and its parallel funds ("H&F", US) acquires within the meaning of Article
        3(1)(b) of the Merger Regulation sole control of the whole of Nets A/S ("Nets", Denmark).
        The concentration is accomplished by way of public bid announced on 25 September
        2017.3
2.      The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
        <U+F02D>     for H&F: private equity firm with offices in San Francisco, New York and London.
              H& F has investments in a variety of sectors worldwide.
        <U+F02D>     for Nets: provision of payments, cards, and information services in Denmark, Norway,
              Sweden, Finland, and Estonia.
3.      After examination of the notification, the European Commission has concluded that the
        notified operation falls within the scope of the Merger Regulation and of paragraph 5(b) of
        the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of certain concentrations
        under Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004.4
