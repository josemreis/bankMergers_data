1.     On 12 May 2014, the European Commission received notification of a proposed
       concentration pursuant to Article 4 of the Merger Regulation by which the undertakings
       Penske Automotive Group Inc ("PAG", USA) and Ibericar, Sociedad Ibérica del Automóvil
       S.A. ("Ibericar", Spain ) acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Merger
       Regulation joint control over Ibericar Keldenich S.L.("Ibericar K", Spain) and the BMW's car
       dealership business in Spain ("Car dealership business") by way of purchase of assets2.
2.     The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
          -        for PAG: automobile dealership business including sale, maintenance and repair
                   of motor vehicles and other ancillary products such as finance and insurance
                   products in North America, the UK, Germany and Italy;
          -        for Ibericar: retail sale of new cars, used cars, aftersales, paint and repair, parts
                   and distribution of financial products in Spain;

      -       for Ibericar K: car retailing and related activities in Spain;
      -       for the Car dealership business: car retailing and ancillary activities in Spain.
3. After examination of the notification, the European Commission has concluded that the
   notified operation falls within the scope of the Merger Regulation and of paragraph 5(c) of
   the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of certain concentrations
   under Council Regulation (EC) No 139/20043.
4. For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the European Commission
   has decided not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the
   internal market and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of
   Article 6(1)(b) of the Merger Regulation.
