1.      On 29 April 2015, the European Commission received notification of a proposed
        concentration pursuant to Article 4 of the Merger Regulation by which IDeA Capital Funds
        SGR S.p.A. (“IDeA”, Italy), IP Investimenti e Partecipazioni S.r.l. (“IP”, Italy), Hunt
        Capital S.A.(“Hunt”, Luxembourg) acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the
        Merger Regulation, joint control of Corin Group PLC (“Corin”, United Kingdom), by way
        of contract of management in the form of modified shareholders' agreement.3
