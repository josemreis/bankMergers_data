Subject:      Case No. COMP/M.3103 – General Electric Consumer Finance /Abbey
              National
              Notification of 26.02.2003 pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC)
              No. 4064/891
              Publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities No. C 52,
              06/03/2003, page 12
1.  On 26.02.2003, the Commission received notification of a proposed concentration pursuant
    to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89 by which the US undertaking
    General Electric Company (“GE”) acquires within the meaning of article 3(1)(b) of the
    Council control of parts of the UK undertaking Abbey National Plc (“Abbey National”) by
    way of purchase of assets.
2.      The business activities of the undertakings concerned are :
        -        for GE : diversified company active in various manufacturing, technology and
                 services business activities, including financial services;
        -        for Abbey National : provision of personal financing services, including mortgages,
                 savings products, investment products, banking, general insurance and life insurance;

-              for the business to be acquired by GE : provision of secured and unsecured loans.
2.  After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
    operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89 and of paragraph
    4, subparagraph c, of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of
    certain concentrations under Council Regulation (EEC) No 4064/892.
3.  For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the Commission has decided
    not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the common market
    and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of Article 6(1)(b) of
    Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89.
