1.   On 02.08.2005, the Commission received notification of a proposed concentration pursuant
     to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/2004 by which WestLB AG (“WestLB”,
     Germany), Norddeutsche Landesbank Girozentrale AöR (“NordLB”, Germany), Shinsei
     Bank Ltd. (“Shinsei”, Japan) and J.C.Flowers & Co., LLC (“Flowers”, USA) acquire within
     the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of SGK
     Servicegesellschaft Kreditmanagement (“SGK”, Germany) by way of purchase of shares in
     a newly created company constituting a joint venture.

2. The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
      -       for WestLB: Central institution of the savings banks of North Rhine-Westphalia
              and Brandenburg and international universal bank,
      -       for NordLB: State Bank for the states of Lower Saxony and Saxony-Anhalt and
              international universal bank,
      -       for Shinsei: investment and retail bank,
      -       for Flowers: private equity investor,
      -       for SGK: acquisition, administration and servicing of loans.
3. After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
   operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/2004 and of paragraph
   5, subparagraph c, of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of
   certain concentrations under Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/20042.
4. For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the Commission has decided
   not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the common market
   and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of Article 6(1)(b) of
   Council Regulation (EC) No. 139/2004.
