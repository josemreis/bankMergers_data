              Commission decision pursuant to Article 6(1)(b) of Council Regulation (EC) No
                         1
              139/2004
1.  On 22.11.2013, the European Commission received notification of a proposed
    concentration pursuant to Article 4 of the Merger Regulation by which the undertaking
    Apollo EPF II Partnership, managed by Apollo EPF Advisors II, L.P., which itself is part of
    Apollo Management, L.P. ("Apollo", USA ) acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b)
    of the Merger Regulation control of the whole of Altamira Santander Real Estate
    Distribucion, S.A. ("Altamira", Spain) by way of purchase of shares.
2.  The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
        - for Apollo: portfolio investments,
        - for Altamira: provision of debt management services.2
3.  After examination of the notification, the European Commission has concluded that the
    notified operation falls within the scope of the Merger Regulation and of paragraph 5(c) of
