Dear Sir or Madam,
1.      On 18 May 2018, the European Commission received notification of a proposed
        concentration pursuant to Article 4 of the Merger Regulation by which HPS Investment
        Partners, LLC ("HPS", USA) and Madison Dearborn Partners, LLC ("MDP", USA)
        acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Merger Regulation joint control of the
        whole of Professional Fee Protection Limited ("PFP", United Kingdom) by way of
        purchase of shares.3
2.      The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
        <U+F02D>     HPS is a US-based investment firm with a focus on acquisitions and strategic
              financing or recapitalisation of companies that require financial assistance;
        <U+F02D>     MDP is a US-based private equity investment firm;
        <U+F02D>     PFP is a small UK provider of risk management services and tax advisory services.
3.      After examination of the notification, the European Commission has concluded that the
        notified operation falls within the scope of the Merger Regulation and of paragraph 5(c) of
