(1)     On 22/09/2008, the Commission received notification of a proposed concentration
        pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 by which the undertakings
        Aegon Spanje ("Aegon Spanje", Spain) controlled by Aegon N.V. ("Aegon", the
        Netherlands) and Caixa d’Estalvis de Terrassa (“Caixa Terrassa”, Spain) acquire within
        the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of CaixaTerrassa
        Vida ("CaixaTerrassa Vida", Spain), which is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Caixa
        Terrassa, by way of purchase of shares.
(2)     The business activities of the undertakings concerned are :
        -     Aegon Spanje :                insurance and pension products in Spain;
        -     Aegon :                       insurance, pension and related products world-wide;
        -     Caixa Terrassa:               banking operations and insurance distribution in Spain;

    -    CaixaTerrassa Vida:    life and accident insurance and pension products in Spain.
(3) After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
    operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 and of
    paragraph 5(c) of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of
    certain concentrations under Council Regulation (EC) No 139/20042.
(4) For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the Commission has
    decided not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the
    common market and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of
    Article 6(1)(b) of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004.
