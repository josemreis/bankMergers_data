1.   On 20/05/2009 the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration
     pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 by which the undertaking
     Crédit Agricole, S.A. ("Crédit Agricole", France) acquires within the meaning of Article
     3(1) (b) of the Council Regulation control over part of the undertaking CACEIS SAS
     ("CACEIS", France), controlled by Natixis and Crédit Agricole, by way of purchase of
     shares.
2.   The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
        -        for Crédit Agricole: is active in banking and insurance services;
-                for CACEIS: is currently a joint venture between Natixis and Crédit Agricole that
                 is active in fund custody and fund administration for institutional clients.
3.   After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
     operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 and of paragraph
