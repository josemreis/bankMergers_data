Subject: Case No. COMP/M. 2167 – Citigroup/Associates
             Notification of 10.10.2000 pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC) No.
             4064/891
             Publication in the Official Journal of the European Communities No. C 299/02 -
             20/10/2000.
1.     On 10.10.2000, the Commission received notification of a proposed concentration pursuant
       to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89 by which the US undertaking
       Citigroup Inc. ("Citigroup") acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council
       Regulation control of the whole of the US undertaking Associates First Capital Corporation
       ("Associates") by way of purchase of shares. The business activities of the undertakings
       concerned are :
       - for Citigroup : retail banking; commercial banking; insurance and related services.
       - for Associates : retail banking; commercial banking; insurance and related services.

2.  After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
    operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89 and of paragraph
    4 (c) of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of certain
    concentrations under Council Regulation (EEC) No 4064/892.
3.  For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the Commission has decided
    not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the common market
    and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of Article 6(1)(b) of
    Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89.
