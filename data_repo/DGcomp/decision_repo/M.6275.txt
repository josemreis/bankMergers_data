              Commission decision pursuant to Article 6(1)(b) of Council Regulation (EC) No
                          1
              139/2004
1.  On 23/06/2011, the European Commission received notification of a proposed
    concentration pursuant to Article 4 of the Merger Regulation by which the undertaking BNP
    Paribas S.A. (“BNPP”, France), through its fully owned subsidiary Cardif Assicurazioni
    S.p.A. (“Cardif Assicurazioni”, Italy), acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the
    Merger Regulation sole control of the whole of the undertaking BNL Vita S.p.A. (“BNL
                                                        2
    Vita”, Italy) by way of purchase of shares .
2.  The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
        -        for BNPP: banking and insurance;
        -        for BNL Vita: life insurance in Italy.
3.  After examination of the notification, the European Commission has concluded that the
    notified operation falls within the scope of the Merger Regulation and of paragraph 5(d) of
