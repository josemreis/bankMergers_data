 Subject: Case No. COMP/M.3214 - American Express Financial Corporation / Threadneedle
              Notification of 18.08.2003 pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC)
              No. 4064/891
              Publication in the Official Journal of the European Union No. C 198,
              23.08.2003, page 14
1.   On 18.08.2003, the Commission received notification of a proposed concentration pursuant
     to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89 by which the undertaking American
     Express Financial Corporation ("AEFC"), controlled by the American Express Company
     (USA), acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the
     whole of the undertaking Threadneedle Asset Management Holdings Limited (UK) by way of
     purchase of shares.
2.   The business activities of the undertakings concerned are:
        -        AEFC: travel-related services, financial advisory services and international banking
                 services

       -       Threadneedle: supply of investment products to European retail and institutional
               investors
3.  After examination of the notification, the Commission has concluded that the notified
    operation falls within the scope of Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89 and of paragraph
    4, subparagraph (c), of the Commission Notice on a simplified procedure for treatment of
    certain concentrations under Council Regulation (EEC) No 4064/892.
4.  For the reasons set out in the Notice on a simplified procedure, the Commission has decided
    not to oppose the notified operation and to declare it compatible with the common market
    and with the EEA Agreement. This decision is adopted in application of Article 6(1)(b) of
    Council Regulation (EEC) No. 4064/89.
